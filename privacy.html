Our Privacy & Security Commitment

1. Data Collection

IQ5 collects Amazon SP-API data only from authorized users who explicitly grant access. Unauthorized access is strictly forbidden and may result in immediate suspension and legal action.

2. Data Usage

Data is processed internally to provide analytics, pricing optimization, inventory management, and advertising insights. Data is never sold or shared with third parties.

3. Data Security

  • Encryption in transit and at rest ensures complete protection.
  • Access is restricted based on job responsibilities.
  • Credentials are never hardcoded or shared publicly.
  • Network security prevents unauthorized access attempts.
  • Protocols are regularly reviewed to meet industry best practices.

4. Incident Response

Any unauthorized access, breach attempt, or security incident is immediately reported to security@amazon.com and mitigated according to best practices.

5. Legal Compliance

We comply with Amazon SP-API policies, Acceptable Use Policy, GDPR, and other applicable data protection laws. Compliance documentation is maintained and audit-ready.

6. User Responsibility

Authorized users must not bypass controls, redistribute data, or use Amazon information for unauthorized purposes. Misuse may result in suspension, termination, or legal consequences.

⚠️ Unauthorized data access or misuse is strictly monitored and reported to Amazon. Legal consequences will follow any violation.
