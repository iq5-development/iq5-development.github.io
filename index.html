The Problem

eCommerce businesses today face rising competition, unpredictable demand, and complex advertising alignment. Without intelligent tools, sellers risk losing revenue due to:

  • ⚖️ Price Volatility

    Constant changes in competitor pricing make it hard to stay profitable without over-discounting.

  • 📦 Inventory Mismanagement

    Overstocking locks capital, understocking loses customers. Both damage long-term growth.

  • 🎯 Misaligned Advertising

    Ad campaigns often push products that are overpriced or out of stock — wasting budgets and trust.

  • 🔎 Lack of Insights

    Most sellers lack the market intelligence to forecast demand and anticipate competitors’ moves.
